Case Study

CRM Ticket Workflow Model

A structured support workflow for tracking customer issues from first contact to resolution, with clear ownership, priority handling, escalation notes, and customer follow-up.

Support Scenario

A SaaS customer reports that an account setting, booking action, or platform workflow is not behaving as expected. The support team needs a clean way to capture the issue, diagnose it, escalate it if needed, and keep the customer updated.

Tools Represented

  • Zendesk or Intercom for ticket handling
  • HubSpot or Salesforce for customer records
  • Internal logs or admin tools for issue checks
  • Google Sheets for reporting and pattern tracking

Workflow

Ticket lifecycle.

01

Intake

Capture customer details, account ID, issue summary, affected workflow, and urgency.

02

Categorize

Classify the request as account, billing, booking, access, integration, or platform issue.

03

Troubleshoot

Reproduce the issue, review visible system behavior, check known causes, and document findings.

04

Escalate

Share a concise technical summary, customer impact, screenshots, steps tried, and expected outcome.

05

Resolve

Confirm the fix, update the case, communicate clearly, and add prevention notes where useful.

Result

What this proves.

This case study shows my ability to manage customer issues in a structured support environment, keep CRM data accurate, reduce back-and-forth during escalation, and communicate technical details in a customer-friendly way.
